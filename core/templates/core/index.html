Artifical intelligence for conversations.

Our artificial intelligence learns to reply to customer service conversations. This allows companies and other organizations to make customer service semi-automatic, saving precious customer service agent time for the hardest queries.

Our deep learning based technology learns to respond to conversations. Our algorithm is fresh out of the lab, and we leverage both proprietary algorithms and data sources.

Prior to True Ai, our team has worked at deep learning labs of Amazon.com and Yoshua Bengio. Their research has been featured in publications such as The New York Times and MIT Technology Review. We are finalizing our prototype under EF, Europe’s largest technical pre-seed accelerator.
